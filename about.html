About

I’m a Mathematics graduate from the University of Warwick and a technical data analyst specialising in automated analytical workflows, high‑reliability reporting, and interactive data tools. I work extensively with R, Python, and SQL to clean, validate, and model large datasets, and I build dashboards and reproducible workflows that make complex analysis accessible to stakeholders.

In my current role as a Research Analyst at the Universities and Colleges Employers Association, I design and deliver end‑to‑end data processes for sector‑wide surveys, develop Shiny dashboards, and model multi‑billion‑pound pay scenarios for over 300,000 higher education staff. My work focuses on accuracy, clarity, and building systems that scale.

I enjoy translating technical requirements into robust solutions, improving data quality, and collaborating across teams to deliver reliable outputs under tight deadlines. Outside of work, I’ve volunteered with the Trussell Trust, supporting food distribution and contributing to process improvements during high‑demand periods.
